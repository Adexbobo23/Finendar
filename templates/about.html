About us

Welcome to FINENDAR

Finendar was launched as a digital product by GlobeWorka in 2024 and as a technology platform to address certain problems associated with audience engagement beit as an educator, trainer, assessor, influencer, researcher, brand/marketing promoter or survey-data analyst while engaging in business activities both in the digital space and otherwise.

Created by Expert with decades of Experience In audience engagement

Finendar is basically a learning enhancement tool that gives the creator the luxury of an ICT unit where running a WBT/CBT becomes easy and at little or zero cost. Setting up a customised test or survey becomes real-time at zero maintenance cost, where control is 100% and need for experts, paper, machines or prints is eliminated completely. With Finendar, rewarding the audience in whatever ways is within the control of the content or project creator and as a recruiter, interviewer, HR consultant, course facilitator assessor or educator, the flexibility of the platform is limitless.

Explore More

Finendar, born from the innovative minds at GlobeWorka in 2024, emerged as a digital solution to revolutionize audience engagement across various sectors such as education, training, entertainment, influencing, assessment, research, marketing, and data analysis. Our mission will empower individuals and organizations to monetize their skills and content, measure impact, and foster audience loyalty.

Finendar allows the user to switch modes and generate income both passively and actively, the opportunity is endless.

Finendar is an HR tool designed to make remote training workshops achieve better results by further simplifying recruitment processes, induction for new recruits and large staff training regardless of distance and on a global scale. It is safe and allows easy integration with other social platforms while security is not compromised. If YouTube, Facebook, tiktok, Twitter(X), WhatsApp or Instagram is the creator's thing then Finendar is here to help achieve more and manage feedbacks towards greater maximisation

Our Mission

Building Sustainable Circular Audience Engagement and Reward System (SCAERS) to drive effectiveness, efficiency and empowerment on a global scale.

Our Vission

Fuelling economic drives through sustainable audience engagement and educational solutions with cutting edge innovations to maximise resources, empower stakeholders and eliminate waste.

Corporate Tagline

Do more, do better.

Our Core Values
  • Leadership: inspiring, motivating, and guiding creative minds towards enhanced and greater productivity.
  • Innovation: Providing cutting-edge solutions to simplify processes and eliminate barriers.
  • Flexibility: Offering limitless possibilities for creators, educators, and recruiters.
  • Effectiveness: Measuring impact and ensuring effectiveness in audience engagement.

With Finendar, creators gain the ability to monetize their content effortlessly, engage their audience effectively, and reward them for their participation. Our platform serves as a versatile learning enhancement tool, facilitating the creation and management of web-based training (WBT) and computer-based training (CBT) with ease and minimal cost.

Unique Features
  • Customized Testing and Surveys: Real-time creation and deployment of tests and surveys without maintenance costs.
  • Audience Reward System: Empowering creators to reward their audience for engagement and loyalty.
  • Integrated HR Solutions: Simplifying remote training, recruitment, and induction processes for organizations worldwide.
